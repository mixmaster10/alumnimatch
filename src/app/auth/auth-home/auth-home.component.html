Welcome to AlumniMatch!
AlumniMatch instantly matches and connects you with other college alumni in a free and easy to use app! Interact within a private community of people that went to the same college(s) as you did!

All Members are matched based on shared:
  • Past college degree
  • Family life
  • Hobbies and interests
  • Career and work goals
  • Pet ownership
  • Spirituality
  • Past student organizations
Imagine the alumni you will meet!

AlumniMatch never shares your information outside of the platform, and members can easily remove their account anytime. Also, members can make specific profile answers invisible to everyone or block users completely for privacy protection.
